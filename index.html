World-class Campus to Bring Out the Best in U.

The AI & Robotics Studio at Woxsen University aims to further discovery and knowledge creation in data science, data analytics and predictive analytics. The facility is powered with the latest GPUs from NVIDIA and updated IMAC and is well equipped to run complex algorithms built on NLP, Keras, and TensorFlow.

The Analytics & Behavioural Lab at Woxsen University is a platform for students, scholars, researchers and practitioners to collaborate and draw meaningful and reliable insights through business data analysis for a better future.

The Bloomberg Finance Lab at Woxsen University is exclusively designed for Finance aspirants. With 20 terminals, the lab provides students with a gateway to streaming Finance news across the globe.

The Design & Tech Labs at Woxsen University provide a creative environment and expert support for students to develop, experiment, and collaborate on projects. The state-of-the-art, cutting-edge research facilities are equipped with industry-standard advanced technology and promote applied learning and innovation.

The Woxsen Trade Tower is an incubator where students pitch ideas and progress through a multi-level start-up process, with each level representing a passage through the incubation process. The ideas that successfully pass through all the stages have the opportunity to seek funding from members of the Woxsen Fund, an investment corpus.
